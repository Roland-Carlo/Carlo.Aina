Calm and happy—two words to describe our love. It wasn’t about grand gestures or public declarations of love like those in the movies, but the kind of love that sits with you at dawn and stays until dusk.

A few years ago, we casually met at work. Two individuals with completely different personalities ended up working together on the same team. Little did we know that, rather than being so different, we shared the same values and aspirations.

That was when we discovered a profound connection. From enjoying a homemade sandwich for breakfast near the Bustos Waterway, taking an afternoon market run, indulging in street food, and hunting for the perfect coffee shop to discovering a shared love for food and nature trips, we knew we had found something special—a love that embodies our similarities and transcends our differences.

Through the highs and lows, we have grown together, building a love that is fun, steady, and unwavering. And now, we can’t wait to start the next chapter of our journey—with all of you by our side.
